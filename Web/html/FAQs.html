Returns

You may exchange merchandise purchased at our shop. Outlet purchases must be returned to the outlet. All returned merchandise must be in the original condition when sold and accompanied by the original proof of purchase.

If you are not completely satisfied with your purchase, simply return the item(s) to us in their original condition within 28 days of receipt.

Please allow 7-10 working days from receipt of your goods at our warehouse for the return to be processed. You will receive a confirmation email once your return has been processed with a new tracking number for exchanged goods or a refund receipt.

Care & Repair

Please contact our customer service or refer directly to the retailer you bought the item from.

Warranties are valid for one years. Our limited warranties cover all manufacturing and mechanical defects, excluding malfunction or deterioration resulting from accidents or negligence.

Authorized service centres listed in the warranty booklet will, at their discretion, repair or replace any component needed to restore the timepiece to full working order. Complete warranty information is provided in the booklet.

Counterfeit products are replicas or imitations of authentic products that are aimed to mislead consumers into believing they are genuine goods. Counterfeit products are of poor quality, but are sometimes offered for sale at high prices in order to mislead online shoppers. Such products lack the quality, attention to detail and craftsmanship which characterize authentic goods.

In order to report to suspected counterfeit products, please send an e-mail to us. Please provide us with as much descriptive information as possible about the product or the website, such as the location of the store, the seller of the product, the type of product, the website address, etc. We appreciate your help in our fight against counterfeiting.
